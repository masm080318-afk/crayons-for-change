FAQ

Common Questions

Is the STEM camp really free?

Yes — completely free. No fees, no materials costs. Our goal is to remove every financial barrier so all students can participate.

Who can volunteer?

We welcome volunteers of all ages. Students, parents, and community members can all get involved through our volunteer form.

How do I donate supplies instead of money?

Email us at hello@crayonsforchange.org to arrange a supply drop-off or drive at your location.

What grade levels does the camp serve?

Our camps are designed for elementary and middle school students. Reach out if you have questions about a specific grade level.

How are donations used?

100% of donations go directly toward student programs — supply kits, camp materials, and outreach costs. We are a volunteer-run organization with no paid staff.
