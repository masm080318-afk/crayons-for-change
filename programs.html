Our Programs

From school supply drives to free STEM camps and global outreach — here's how we put education into action.

Our Mission

Expanding access to education for every student

Crayons for Change is dedicated to removing barriers to education by supporting students both locally and globally. We inspire curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Program 1

School Supply Drives

We collect and deliver essential classroom items to partner schools, making sure every student walks in with what they need to succeed — not just on the first day, but all year long.

  • Crayons, markers, colored pencils, and art supplies
  • Notebooks, folders, and loose-leaf paper
  • Backpacks and pencil cases
  • Rulers, scissors, glue sticks, and other classroom essentials
  • International deliveries — including Ahmedabad, India

696+ supplies delivered

And counting. Every drive brings us closer to reaching every student who needs support.

Program 2

Free STEM Summer Camp

Our STEM Summer Camp is completely free — no fees, no barriers. Students get hands-on experience in science, technology, engineering, and math through project-based challenges and collaborative experiments.

What campers learn

  • Basic coding and computational thinking
  • Engineering design through hands-on building challenges
  • Introduction to electronics and simple circuits
  • Problem-solving and teamwork
  • Creative STEM projects and science experiments
Register for Camp Volunteer at Camp

How to Register

Spots are limited. Complete the form early to secure your place.

  1. Open the registration form and select a camp session.
  2. Enter student details — name, grade, and parent or guardian contact info.
  3. Submit to receive a confirmation email with next steps and a welcome checklist.

Registration may close early when sessions reach capacity.

Program 3

International Outreach

Our mission doesn't stop at the local level. We've traveled to Ahmedabad, India to hand-deliver school supplies directly to students in underserved communities.

Direct delivery

Supplies go directly to students in communities with limited access to educational resources.

Student-to-student

Our founder team personally delivers supplies, creating a direct connection between giving and receiving students.

Growing reach

We're actively exploring more international partnerships to expand our impact further each year.

Support these programs

Your donation, volunteer hours, or partnership directly funds supplies, camp materials, and student outreach.
