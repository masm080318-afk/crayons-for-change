OUR STORY

Founded in 7th grade with one belief: no student should fall behind because they can't afford a crayon.

What started as a local supply drive has grown into a movement — free STEM camps, international outreach to Ahmedabad, and community partnerships — run entirely by high school students from Folsom, CA.

"We believe art and education are rights, not privileges — and we're proving it."
