About Us

Who We Are

🏫

High School Students

We're passionate high schoolers from Folsom, California, proving that young people can lead powerful change. Our age is our strength — youth-driven energy and fresh ideas fuel everything we do.

📍

Proudly From Folsom, CA

Born and raised in Folsom, California, we draw deep inspiration from our local community. We're not waiting until we're older — we're making an impact right here, right now.

🎨

Art as a Vehicle

We believe creativity is one of the most powerful forces for change. Crayons and art supplies aren't just tools — they're bridges that connect children to hope, expression, and healing.

"Two high schoolers from Folsom with a box of crayons and a dream to change the world — one drawing at a time."
Our Mission

What We Do

01

Community Outreach

We connect with schools, shelters, and organizations in Folsom and surrounding areas — spreading creativity and providing art supplies to those who need them most.

02

Art Supply Drives

We organize crayon and art supply donation drives so every child has access to the tools they need to express themselves and explore their imagination.

03

Awareness Campaigns

Through social media and local events, we amplify the message that art is a fundamental tool for childhood development, therapy, and community connection.

04

Youth Empowerment

As high schoolers ourselves, we show that age is no barrier to impact. We inspire other young people in Folsom and beyond to stand up and make a difference.
