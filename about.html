The Beginning

A student-led mission to close the education gap

Crayons for Change was founded in 7th grade by Mohammed Mansuri with a simple belief: no student should fall behind because they can't afford a crayon.

What started as a local school supply drive has grown into a full nonprofit running free STEM camps, international outreach, and community partnerships. Today, our leadership team is made up entirely of high school students who give their time, energy, and creativity to serve classrooms near and far.

Access

Providing classroom supplies to partner schools so students can start each day ready to learn.

Curiosity

Sparking a love of learning through hands-on STEM workshops, creativity labs, and coding projects.

Community

Partnering with families, schools, and local businesses to support students year-round.

The Team

Meet the Founders

Four student leaders turning a shared vision into real impact.

Mohammed Aamish Mansuri

President & Co-Founder

Mohammed founded Crayons for Change in 7th grade with a passion for making education accessible to every student. As president and webmaster, he oversees programs and manages the organization's online presence.

Avyukth Salver

Co-President & Co-Founder

Avyukth leads alongside Mohammed, helping drive the organization's strategic direction and expanding Crayons for Change's reach to more students and communities.

Abhinav Madallapali

Vice President & Co-Founder

Advaith Battepati

Secretary & Co-Founder
