Option 1

Make a Donation

Every dollar goes toward putting supplies into students' hands, funding camp materials, and covering the costs that keep our programs free and accessible.

Your donation helps fund:

  • Classroom supply kits (backpacks, notebooks, crayons, etc.)
  • STEM camp materials — electronics kits, science supplies, craft materials
  • Printing and outreach costs for community programs
  • International shipping for global supply deliveries
Donate Now

What your donation covers

$5 Fills one student's pencil case
$15 Covers a full supply kit
$30 Sponsors one camp session slot
$100+ Funds supplies for a full classroom
Option 2

Volunteer With Us

Whether you can give a few hours or commit to a whole camp session, your time makes a real difference.

Supply Drives

Help organize collection events, sort donated items, assemble supply kits, and coordinate deliveries to partner schools.

STEM Camp Support

Assist with camp activities in person or virtually — help students with projects, manage materials, or mentor small groups.

Community Events

Support outreach events, tables at local fairs, and fundraising drives that spread awareness about our mission.

Option 3

Partner With Us

Schools, businesses, and community organizations can partner with Crayons for Change to amplify impact in tangible ways.

For Schools

  • Host a supply drive with your students or staff
  • Receive donated supply kits for students in need
  • Invite us to run a STEM workshop or camp session on campus
  • Collaborate on community education events

For Businesses

  • Sponsor a set of student supply kits
  • Set up a donation or collection box at your location
  • Provide in-kind support — printing, materials, space
  • Co-sponsor a camp session or community event
